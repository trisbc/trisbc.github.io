CertPathway

Overview

CertPathway exists to try to make the task of finding higher-paid career opportunities more available for people who don't have, or don't want, a traditional four-year college degree. This is accomplished by providing connecting users to certification programs, short-term educational programs, or any other relevant programs that can be used to help them qualify for higher-paid positions either in their current industry or in another industry of interest.

To match people with programs that fit their needs, we take a simple interest form and their resume (if submitted) and use those to match users to programs that exist within our database.
